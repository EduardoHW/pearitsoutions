Analysis: These are cookies that allow the person responsible for them to monitor and analyze the behavior of users of the websites to which they are linked, including quantifying the impact of advertisements. Behavioural advertising: These are cookies that store information on user behavior obtained through continuous observation of their browsing habits, and which allow the development of a specific profile to display personalized advertising based on this. In other words, they are cookies that allow the effective management of advertising spaces that have been included on the website, normally by third parties that manage the advertising spaces and that allow the content of the advertising to be adapted so that it is relevant to the user and to avoid showing advertisements that the user has already seen, as well as limiting the number of advertising impacts that we make on each user. Functionality or personalization: These are cookies that allow information to be remembered so that the user accesses the service with certain characteristics that can differentiate their experience from that of other users, such as the language, the number of results to be displayed when a user performs a search, the appearance or content of the website depending on the type of browser through which the user accesses or the region from which they access, etc. Deactivating these cookies may mean that the browsing experience is less functional for the user.
